# HeadsUp AI — Family & Friends Test

The goal is not to prove the app is good. The goal is to find what is confusing, wrong, slow or actually useful.

## 10-minute test

1. Open the Home screen and explain what you think the app does without anyone helping you.
2. Open one overdue/coming-up task and mark a pretend job complete.
3. Ask the AI one real maintenance question in your own words.
4. Ask one vague question, such as “my pool isn’t sucking right” or “something keeps chirping.”
5. Try a question the app probably does **not** know.
6. Open Photo Scout only if you are on Wi-Fi and are willing to test the larger model download.
7. Change the home name or your name in Settings.
8. Look at the 90-day cost forecast and say whether it feels useful or annoying.
9. Go to the **Test** tab and rate usefulness, ease and trust.
10. Write what you would change first.

## Questions worth testing

- My pool vacuum has weak suction. What should I check?
- My pool filter pressure is high.
- My smoke detector keeps chirping.
- How often should I change my AC filter?
- My dryer is taking longer to dry clothes.
- What maintenance should I do before summer?
- My tire pressure light came on.
- I smell propane. What should I do?

## What we care about most

- Did the app understand normal everyday wording?
- Was the first suggested task actually relevant?
- Did the answer feel safe and trustworthy?
- Was the amount of information right?
- Would you use the reminder/forecast part again?
- What would make you delete the app?

Use the in-app **Test** tab after each tester session. Export the JSON periodically so feedback is not lost if browser storage is cleared.
